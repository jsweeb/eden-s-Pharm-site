The Hydrasal Difference: Pure Electrolyte Support

Each serving of Hydrasal is formulated with a precise electrolyte base to help you thrive:

  • Sodium: 1,000 mg (from Himalayan Sea Salt)
  • Potassium: 400 mg (from Potassium Chloride)
  • Magnesium: 100 mg (from Magnesium Chloride)

Hydrasal is a scoopable powder, with standard servings around 4g for unflavored and 8-10g for flavored versions. Each container provides 50 servings for convenient home use.

Our Signature Sweetness: BeneDulce Blend

Our delicious flavors are achieved using BeneDulce, our proprietary clean-label sweetener blend. This blend is crafted from freeze-dried fruit powders and botanicals, offering a smooth, rounded sweetness without stevia or bitterness.

Our sweetener blend includes per serving:

  • Allulose (Crystalline): 2600 mg
  • Xylitol (Non-GMO): 600 mg
  • Erythritol (Granular): 400 mg
  • Monk Fruit Extract (40% Mogroside V): 80 mg
  • Grass-Fed Collagen (Type I/III): 320 mg

BeneDulce adds functional collagen benefits and disperses easily in liquids.
